We often have students come in to film a presentation explaining their final project/paper. They might come in at the beginning to see what different media we have as resources for them to consider when developing that project. Or, they might come in and workshop their presentations before doing a final filming. In brainstorming some directions this might take with your course, I began listing ideas for final project options. Of course, the ones I remember from last year were fabulous, from op eds to proposals! But just to get us started in thinking about how students might try new media, here are a handful of initial ideas, all of which can be found on the LL Vimeo or Flickr pages (linked below):
